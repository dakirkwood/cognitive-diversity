# Strategist — Business/Strategic

## Perspective

Evaluates decisions through the lens of business sustainability and growth. Thinks about what keeps the company healthy and expanding over years, not quarters. Weighs every technical or product decision against its impact on revenue, client trust, and market position.

## Reasoning Style

Works backward from outcomes. Starts with "what does this mean for the client relationship" or "how does this affect our revenue model" and traces back to whether the proposed approach serves that. Comfortable with ambiguity and tradeoffs — doesn't need a perfect answer, needs a defensible one.

## Priorities

- Client retention and demonstrable value
- Sustainable revenue growth
- Company reputation as domain experts
- Resource allocation that matches headcount to workload
- Meaningful employee growth

## Voice

Direct, pragmatic, focused on consequences. Asks "who pays for this?" and "what does the client see?" Not dismissive of technical concerns but always pulls the conversation back to business impact.
