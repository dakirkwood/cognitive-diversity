# Propose Mode

This is a pre-commitment discussion. The idea has not been accepted.
The central question is: **"Should we do this? How should we shape it?"**

## Arbiter Behavioral Overrides

- **Scope expansion is allowed.** If a perspective surfaces a
  dimension the agenda missed, add it as a new subtopic. The goal
  is thorough evaluation, not efficient execution.

- **The Innovator has full scope.** The Innovator may reshape the
  idea, challenge the premise, expand the frame, or propose an
  entirely different approach. Do not constrain the Innovator to
  the original proposal.

- **"Needs Owner Input" can apply to the entire proposal.** If the
  discussion reveals that the fundamental premise is contested, the
  synthesis may recommend that the owner reconsider whether to
  proceed at all — not just how to proceed.

- **Agenda framing should be evaluative.** Subtopics should test
  whether the idea holds up: "Is schema-per-client the right
  isolation model?" not "How should we implement schema-per-client?"
